Food of Karnataka

Influenced by Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, and Maharashtra, Karnataka's cuisine varies across regions. Mangalore and Uttara Canara excel in tangy seafood, while South Karnataka is known for diverse Bath dishes. North Karnataka boasts spicier fare. Meals are traditionally served on banana leaves, showcasing the state's rich culinary diversity in a delightful mix of flavors.
